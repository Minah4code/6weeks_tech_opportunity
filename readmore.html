Kwara Tech

Kwaratech Academy is pleased to announce a 6-week tech opportunity for youths and corp members who are interested in developing their tech skills and exploring career opportunities in the tech industry. This program is designed to provide participants with practical skills that are in high demand in today's digital world.

This program is open to recent graduates, and young professionals who are looking to switch careers or enhance their existing skills. The program will run for 6 weeks, with classes held thrice a week in the evenings.

The program will cover a wide range of topics, Content Creation and Copywriting, Product Management, Tech for Accounting & Finance graduates, Digital Marketing, Power BI and more. Participants will learn from experienced tech instructors who will provide hands-on training in these areas. They will have the opportunity to work on real-world projects and receive feedback from industry professionals.

By participating in this program, participants will gain valuable experience and skills that will help them stand out in a competitive job market. They will also have the opportunity to network with industry professionals and potentially land internships or job offers. Additionally, participants will receive a certificate of completion that they can add to their resumes or LinkedIn profiles.

To register for this program, please visit our website at www.kwaratech.com. Space is limited, so be sure to register early to secure your spot. If you have any questions or need further information, please contact us at 07069272326.

We believe that this program will help participants unlock their potential and pursue exciting opportunities in the tech industry. We look forward to welcoming a new cohort of tech enthusiasts and helping them achieve their goals.
